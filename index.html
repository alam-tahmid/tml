About Us

Transmarine Logistics Ltd. (TML) is a total supply chain management company under the flagship of MGH. We provide customized supply chain solutions from a wide range of services including Sea freight, Air Freight, Inland Haulage, Pick and Pack, QC and warehousing, all tailored to the need of our clients.

We are the pioneer of Multi-Vendor Origin Pick and Pack for G-Star in South-Asia and bypass the need for destination distribution center. Besides providing a real time web tracking solution from finishing line to DC, we provide professional & experienced staff who ensure quality service to the clients.

Facts About Us

COMPLETE LOGISTICS SOLUTION

Transmarine Logistics Pvt. Ltd. offers a complete logistics solution for supply chain requirements. Our services include warehousing, order picking/packing as well as shipping and air freight forwarding.

SHIPPING AT EASE

Our Sea Freight services provide efficient equipment utilization with secured bonded area wherein no record of pilferage, theft or damage has been reported. Besides specializing in following stuffing SOP: Item, Color, Assortment, Size, Pallet wise stuffing as instructed by the consignee, pictures are also taken at various stages of stuffing for complete visibility.

AIR IT QUICK AND SAFE

Our Air Freight services provide guaranteed uplift at guaranteed rates. With first privately owned bonded warehouse, we ensure zero damage to the merchandise and fastest acceptance/shipment process as well as ensure quick stock replenishment. We are proud to own top charter flight operation out of DAC.


INLAND TRUCKING FROM FACTORY TO PORT

Our inland haulage focuses on timely delivery from factory to port at fixed rates throughout the year. Equipped with modern GPS tracking system, it ensures availability during festivals and peaks. It also covers transit insurance from factory door to the port.

SAFE KEEPING AT WAREHOUSE

Transmarine Logistics Pvt. Ltd. owns bonded warehouse spread across 55,000 sq. ft. area with 45,000 sq. ft. warehousing area. Our "Warehouse Management System" optimizes labour productivity and increases the efficiency of warehouse operations.

PICK & PACK and Q.C. PLATFORM

Transmarine Logistics Pvt. Ltd. has recorded a peak productivity of 81,000 pieces/day. The packing is completely error free. The Q.C. Platform is spread across 51 acre area.
